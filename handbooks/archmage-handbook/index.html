The Archmage Codex

A vintage 5e-style handbook of the great wizard names that still shake worlds: Elminster, Mordenkainen, Khelben, Szass Tam, Alustriel, Laeral, Karsus, Vecna, Iggwilv, Bigby, and Gale of Waterdeep. Each profile is presented in 5e format, with official 5e material used where it exists and lore-faithful 5e conversion work used where the game never printed a full modern sheet.

5e format throughout Profile art for every wizard Official spellbooks plus legacy signatures Attire, relics, towers, sanctums, and lairs
Legacy signatures are separated from current 5e spellbook entries so the profiles stay rules-clean without losing history.
